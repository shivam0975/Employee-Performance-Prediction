About This Project

Project Title: Machine Learning Approach for Employee Performance Prediction

This system uses a machine learning pipeline to analyze and forecast employee productivity in the garment sector. It considers past performance, department roles, overtime, idle time, team structures, and more to make insightful predictions.

Project Objectives

  • Understand performance trends using data-driven techniques
  • Optimize HR processes such as talent retention and resource planning
  • Enhance managerial decision-making through predictive insights

Key Use Cases

  1. Talent Retention: Predict high-performing employees at risk of leaving and take proactive steps
  2. Performance Coaching: Identify employees needing support and recommend training
  3. Smart Allocation: Assign workers to tasks aligned with their productivity trends
